Welcome to MTB Law

A legal consultancy devoted to loyal service and dynamic problem solving

My name is Michael T. Burgner, Esq. I am an attorney, business leader and content creator. Equal parts creative thinker, calculating tactician and diplomatic leader, my professional experience includes more than 10 years in law, business and journalism.

I employ conflict resolution skills and cost-benefit analysis to isolate effective solutions to complex problems. I leverage a classic journalism education and public relations savvy to improve branding with tightly crafted content. And I protect client interests by drafting or interpreting contractual obligations in clear and concise fashion.

Unlike many attorneys, my capabilities extend beyond the law. As a former executive leader at a multinational corporation, I am well-versed in business operations, including process improvement, crisis response and human resources. This formidable combination of legal knowledge and business acumen enables me to provide my clients with a unique perspective.
